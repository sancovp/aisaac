# AIDA Fractal Value Ladder

The master framework for all TWI marketing. Every customer touchpoint — page, blog, email, call, offer — is an instantiation of this structure.

## Core Insight

A Customer Journey is a Hero's Journey from the POV of the accomplished dream AND from the POV of each obstacle/overcome. But a CJ differs from a HJ: in a CJ, the CUSTOMER convinces THEMSELVES to go on the journey with the value ladder BECAUSE THEY ALREADY BELIEVE THEMSELVES TO BE THE HERO, instead of being forced into it.

## The ADHD Trigger Mechanism

The value ladder leverages the ADHD trigger cycle (exploitation of current resource vs foraging for new better one) by creating a gold rush atmosphere out of abundant exchange — framing every transaction as a massive win-win. This unlocks impulsive behavior and makes people forego current patterns in favor of better ones they develop for themselves on the basis of having a map to better resources. The value ladder gives them courage to search AND the map so they won't lose the pokemon battle when they find the resource.

The seller proves the map works because they did it themselves first. The bootstrap loop IS the proof.
