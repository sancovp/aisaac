# Direct Marketing Diet

# No B.S. Direct Marketing: The Direct Marketing Diet

## The Framework

Dan Kennedy's "Direct Marketing Diet" is a 6-step process for planning and executing direct marketing campaigns.

## The 6 Steps

### Step 1: Identify Your Target
Who is your customer? Be specific — demographics, psychographics, buying behavior.

### Step 2: Define the Offer
What are you selling, at what price, with what bonuses and guarantee?

### Step 3: Choose Your Media
Where will you reach this audience? Email, direct mail, paid ads, etc.

### Step 4: Create the Creative
Develop the actual marketing message — ads, emails, sales letters, videos.

### Step 5: Test
Run small-scale tests before full deployment. Measure results rigorously.

### Step 6: Optimize & Scale
Take what works, kill what doesn't, scale the winners.

## Key Principles

### Test Before You Invest
Never spend more on marketing than you've tested and proven.

### Track Everything
Response rate, cost per lead, cost per acquisition, customer lifetime value.

### The Direct Marketing Math
```
Revenue = (List Size) × (Response Rate) × (Average Sale) × (Margin)
```

## Common Mistakes

1. **Skipping testing** — Going full-scale with unproven campaigns
2. **Ignoring numbers** — Not tracking metrics rigorously
3. **Wrong list** — Marketing to people who don't buy
4. **Weak offer** — Not enough value or wrong positioning
5. **Premature scaling** — Scaling before optimization

## Application

For any campaign:
1. Who is the target?
2. What's the offer?
3. Where will you reach them?
4. What's the message?
5. How will you test it?
6. How will you optimize?
