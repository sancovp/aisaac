# Expert Secrets Funnel Types

# expert-secrets-funnel-types

## Core Principle

> "Different funnels serve different purposes at different stages of the Value Ladder."

**The key insight:** Your funnel should match where your prospect is in their journey. Match the funnel to the customer's current level of awareness and commitment.

---

## The Funnel Types Overview

| Funnel Type | Purpose | Where in Value Ladder |
|-------------|---------|---------------------|
| **Lead Squeeze** | Capture leads | Bottom (Attract) |
| **Survey** | Qualify & segment | Transition |
| **Summit** | Build authority | Mid |
| **Book** | Build list & trust | Mid |
| **Reverse** | High-ticket close | Top |

---

## Lead Squeeze Funnel

### What It Is
The simplest funnel — give something free or cheap to capture contact information.

### Structure
```
Landing Page (Free Offer) → Thank You Page → Email Follow-up
```

### When to Use
- Bottom of the Value Ladder
- Cold audience (they don't know you)
- Lead generation for email list

### Examples
- Free ebook/report
- Free checklist/template
- Free training video
- Low-cost trial ($1-$47)

---

## Survey Funnel

### What It Is
Instead of a squeeze page, prospects answer questions that segment them.

### Structure
```
Survey Page → Segment-Specific Offer → Follow-up Sequence
```

### Why It Works
> "The survey pre-qualifies them AND delivers a personalized offer in one step."

### When to Use
- When your audience has different needs/segments
- To segment before offering
- To increase conversion by matching offer to segment

### Key Question Types
- "What's your biggest challenge with X?"
- "Where are you currently in Y?"
- "What's your goal with Z?"

---

## Summit Funnel

### What It Is
A multi-day live (or replayed) event that builds authority and trust.

### Structure
```
Registration → Sessions → Live Pitch → Upsell
```

### Why It Works
> "A summit creates a 'communal experience' where the group moves through the journey together."

### Key Elements
- Multiple speakers (experts + you)
- Urgency (live event, limited time)
- Community element
- Live Q&A
- Pitch at the end (or woven throughout)

### When to Use
- Mid-ladder (building authority)
- When you have partners/affiliates to promote
- To build a community around your movement

---

## Book Funnel

### What It Is
Give away a book (physical or digital) in exchange for contact info + upsell.

### Structure
```
Book Offer → Tripwire → Continuity → High-Ticket
```

### Why It Works
- Books create authority and trust
- Low cost to produce, high perceived value
- Filters for serious prospects

### The Book Funnel Math
```
Book Cost: $3-5
Frontend Price: $9.95 + S&H
Upsell to Continuity: $29-97/mo
Lifetime Value: $300-1000+
```

---

## Reverse Funnel

### What It Is
Instead of building a funnel to sell, you reverse-engineer from the customer's desired outcome.

### Structure
```
Customer Desired Outcome ← Work backwards ← Funnel Steps
```

### The Reverse Process
1. Define the end result (what they want)
2. Identify what needs to happen BEFORE they buy
3. Build the path TO that point
4. Create the funnel backwards

---

## The Perfect Webinar (Micro-Funnel)

### The Structure
```
Pre-frame → Hook → Story → Presentation → Offer → Close
```

### Pre-frame (Before Webinar)
- "This is not a pitch"
- Establish the rules
- Create curiosity

### The 9-Step Close
1. **Hook** — Grab attention
2. **Story** — Epiphany Bridge
3. **Presentation** — Show the system
4. **Offer** — Present the solution
5. **Scarcity** — Limited time/spots
6. **Urgency** — Action NOW
7. **Bonus** — Extra incentive
8. **Guarantee** — Remove risk
9. **Close** — "Click the button below"

## Funnel Selection Framework

### What to Ask

| Question | Funnel Type |
|----------|-------------|
| Cold audience, just capturing leads? | Lead Squeeze |
| Multiple customer segments? | Survey |
| Building authority and community? | Summit |
| Building list with a physical/digital book? | Book |
| High-ticket offer ($1K+)? | Reverse |
| Selling a service/training? | Perfect Webinar |

---

## Key Questions for Funnel Type

/prompts:

- "Where is my prospect in the Value Ladder?"
- "What's my goal for THIS funnel? (Capture, qualify, sell, upsell)"
- "Do I have different customer segments that need different offers?"
- "What's my frontend offer? What's my backend offer?"
- "Should I use a webinar, summit, or direct pitch?"
- "How do I match the funnel to my product/service?"
