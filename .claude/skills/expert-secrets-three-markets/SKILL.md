# Expert Secrets: The Three Core Markets/Desires

## The Framework

Every market and opportunity ultimately serves one of three core human desires:

| Core Market | What People Want | Common Expressions |
|-------------|------------------|-------------------|
| **Health** | Physical wellbeing, longevity | Weight loss, fitness, supplements, medical, anti-aging |
| **Wealth** | Financial freedom, abundance | Make money, invest, retire, business, side hustle |
| **Relationships** | Connection, intimacy, belonging | Dating, marriage, parenting, friendships, community |

## Why These Three?

These three markets map to fundamental human needs:
- **Health** = survival instinct (body)
- **Wealth** = resource accumulation (security)
- **Relationships** = tribal belonging (social)

When you strip away the specifics, every "result someone desires" traces back to one of these three.

## Application

### For Positioning Your Offer
Ask: Which core market does my opportunity serve?
- If health → speak to vitality, energy, longevity
- If wealth → speak to freedom, security, abundance
- If relationships → speak to connection, belonging, love

### For Understanding Customer Motivation
The specific result they want (lose 20 lbs, make $10K/mo, fix marriage) is a vehicle for one of these three deeper desires.

### For Creating New Opportunities
If you're struggling to find a market, ask:
- What's broken in the health space?
- What's broken in the wealth space?
- What's broken in the relationships space?

Every innovation eventually serves one of these three.
