# Hormozi Lead Getters

# hormozi-lead-getters

## Core Principle

> "Lead getters give you leverage. Only two people can let strangers know about your stuff — you and other people. And there are more of them than you."

**The key insight:** Instead of doing all the marketing yourself, get OTHER people to advertise for you. This compounds your time and multiplies leads without proportional effort increase.

---

## The Four Lead Getters

### Lead Getter Type 1: Customers
Customers who buy your stuff and tell other people about it.

**How it works:**
- Happy customers refer friends/family
- Word-of-mouth is the best source of new work
- Apply the "hinge method" for referrals

**The Hinge Method:**
1. Ask for a referral
2. Get a three-way introduction
3. Grab the customer's phone, take a picture of the two of you
4. Text that picture to the referral AND your own number
5. For virtual: screenshot a video call and do the same

**Why it works:** Social proof + personal connection = high trust referral.

---

### Lead Getter Type 2: Employees
People inside your business who get you leads.

**How it works:**
- Employees have networks outside work
- They can promote to their contacts
- Incentive structures drive referrals

**Best practices:**
- Train employees on the offer
- Make it easy for them to share
- Align incentives with referral success

---

### Lead Getter Type 3: Agencies
Businesses with services that get you leads.

**How it works:**
- Marketing agencies, PR firms, partner businesses
- They already have audiences
- You pay them to promote your offer

**Examples:**
- SEO agencies promoting to their client base
- Complementary service providers (not competitors)
- Distribution partners with aligned audiences

---

### Lead Getter Type 4: Affiliates
Businesses who tell their audiences about your stuff to get you leads — for a commission.

**How it works:**
- Affiliates promote to THEIR audience
- They get a commission for each sale
- You access markets you couldn't reach alone

**Key example from GymLaunch:**
- Used gym clients as an affiliate network
- Each gym promoted to its members
- Generated cross-referrals between supplement company and gym locations

## The Leverage Ladder

### Scenario 1: You Are the Lead Getter
- You do ALL marketing yourself
- Work: HIGH | Leads: LOW | Leverage: LOW

### Scenario 2: You Get ONE Lead Getter
- One person does marketing for you
- Work: LOW | Leads: LOW | Leverage: HIGH

### Scenario 3: You Get MANY Lead Getters
- Multiple people advertising for you
- Work: HIGH | Leads: HIGH | Leverage: HIGHER

### Scenario 4: Lead Getters Who Get Lead Getters
- Recruiters recruit other recruiters
- Work: LOW | Leads: HIGH | Leverage: HIGHEST

---

## The Core Four Stacking

The core four advertising activities (warm outreach, cold outreach, content, paid ads) stack TWICE:

```
TIER 1: You do the core four → get leads → get lead getters
TIER 2: Lead getters do the core four → get leads on your behalf
```

**The multiplier effect:** Lead getters can go get lead getters.

---

## Lead Getters vs Core Four

| Aspect | Core Four | Lead Getters |
|--------|-----------|--------------|
| What it is | Activities YOU do | People who do activities FOR you |
| Time | You trade time for leads | Others trade time, you don't |
| Leverage | Lower | Higher |
| Scalability | Linear | Exponential |
| Cost | Direct expense | Often commission/percentage |

---

## The Order of Operations

1. **First:** Use core four to get your first customers
2. **Then:** Turn customers into referrers
3. **Then:** Add employees who can promote
4. **Then:** Partner with agencies
5. **Finally:** Build affiliate network
