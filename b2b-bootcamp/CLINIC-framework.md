# CLINIC Framework — The Patient's Journey

The boot camp isn't a boot camp. It's a CLINIC. The acronym IS the experience.

## C — CONSULT / CAPTURE SYMPTOMS
Intake: what hurts, where leads come from, what happens today.

## L — LOOK INSIDE
Run the scan: calls, CRM, response time, follow-up, routing.

## I — IDENTIFY CONDITION
Name the pathology: lead decay, missed-call leakage, SDR inconsistency, routing drag.

## N — NARRATE TRAJECTORY
Show what happens if untreated: lost revenue, rising CAC, staff burnout, competitor capture.

## I — INTERVENE
Prescribe treatment: AI employee/system tier.

## C — CARE PLAN
Implementation, optimization, monitoring, reporting, reinforcement.

## CLINIC vs DOCTOR

| CLINIC | DOCTOR | Perspective |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| The patient's journey | The practitioner's protocol | Same process, two sides |
| C - Consult | D - Diagnose | Patient tells symptoms / Doctor examines |
| L - Look Inside | O - Observe | Patient sees the scan / Doctor reads it |
| I - Identify | C - Consequences | Patient learns the name / Doctor shows trajectory |
| N - Narrate | T - Treat | Patient hears the story / Doctor prescribes |
| I - Intervene | O - Options | Patient receives treatment / Doctor presents choices |
| C - Care Plan | R - Resolve | Patient leaves with plan / Doctor reinforces decision |

## The B2B Psychology

The CLINIC environment creates PROFESSIONAL OBLIGATION, not sales pressure.

After the CLINIC, the prospect thinks:

"I would have to be such a fucking moron not to engage this..."

And in B2B specifically:

"I would have to LITERALLY NOT BE DOING MY JOB to ignore this. Therefore, if someone FOUND OUT that I was presented this offer for OUR BUSINESS and I ignored it, I would look SO STUPID. I can't afford that, so since I'm at work and I wanna keep my job..."

You don't close them. The CLINIC closes them. They close THEMSELVES because the alternative is professional incompetence.

| Context | Anxiety Type | Trigger |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| Consumer (dentist) | Personal health | "My teeth will fall out" |
| B2B (CLINIC) | Professional competence | "My boss will find out I ignored this" |

The CLINIC makes NOT buying the risky decision.

## The C (Consult) Lock Script

The nudge at the start. Restate their job back to them so they can't escape the frame:

"You're here because you don't have AI systems and you want to keep your job, basically, and your job is to look for these opportunities. But your job is specifically to save money, time, and resources for the business while increasing revenue, available labor, and resources for the business (right? isn't it?). So, you specifically are here to see if the business spending money on this is going to benefit it and create some kind of measurable ROI, right? Just making sure."

After they nod, everything that follows is measured against THEIR stated job description. When you show the revenue leak, they can't dismiss it — they just told you their job is to find exactly this. "Just making sure" is the casual lock that makes the rest of the CLINIC inevitable.

## The L (Look Inside) — Other People's Numbers

After the C lock, show them public data as the diagnostic instrument:

"So essentially the numbers you need are [these numbers from other businesses that show what happened when they implemented AI in the past]. Now, notice that those numbers are from [the past] and AI has gotten [this much better on every benchmark since then]... (btw the human levels of these benchmarks are these)"

The triple punch:
1. **Historical proof** — other businesses got X results with AI
2. **Trajectory** — AI has improved Y% since those results were generated
3. **Human baseline** — and here's where humans perform on those same benchmarks

The prospect does the math themselves: "If THOSE businesses got X with WORSE AI... and AI is now Y% better... and humans are at Z..." → back to the C lock: their job is to find exactly this kind of ROI opportunity. Their own math does the closing.

## The I (Identify Condition) — The Zero AI Diagnosis

"So look: if those businesses got those results... Now, your business specifically, how much AI does it have?"

"Zero."

"OK."

That "OK" is the most powerful word in the script. They just admitted they have nothing. Everything after that is pure gap.

### ICP: NON-AI BUSINESSES WITH NO AI YET

"AI Transformations for Non-AI Businesses That Don't Have Any AI Yet"

Why ZERO AI is the ideal target:
- Gap is INFINITE (0 → something). Maximum shock from the numbers.
- No competitor comparison — you're the FIRST. Nobody to evaluate against.
- VOID → SYSTEM is literally true, not metaphor.
- They haven't been burned by bad AI agencies — no trust damage to repair.
- The dentist patient who hasn't been in 10 years — SO much work needed, value is undeniable.
- No BS, non-direct-marketing businesses that just need SYSTEMS.
