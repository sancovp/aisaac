# LIFE — Post-Close Nurture + Fulfillment (The Flywheel)

DOCTOR closes once. LIFE compounds forever.

## L — LOVE

They keep loving everything because you keep running systems. The systems work. The revenue leak stopped. After-hours leads get captured. Follow-up happens automatically. Every day the system runs, they love it more. Love is not an emotion here — it's the natural response to a system that delivers results without them having to think about it.

## I — INTEREST

Prove your level of care by remaining *interested* in them. Check in. Ask how the numbers look. Notice things before they do. "Hey, I noticed your after-hours call volume spiked 30% last week — looks like your ad campaign is working. Want to add capacity?" They remain interested in trusting you because you remain interested in serving them. Interest is bidirectional — your genuine care creates their genuine trust.

## F — FULFILLMENT

Actually do the thing. Deliver the treatment. The systems run. The agents work. The reports come in. This is where the work happens — not promises, not decks, not strategy sessions. The AI employees show up every day and do their job. Fulfillment is the proof that everything you said in DOCTOR was true.

## E — EXCEL

Nurture until they leave a review and ascend. Excel means two things:

1. **They excel** — their business gets measurably better, they have proof, they feel it
2. **They ascend** — they leave a review (social proof for your next PSA), they refer others (new patients), and they ask "what else can you do?" (back to DOCTOR for next tier)

Excel is the flywheel trigger. Every E creates:
- A review → fuels more PSAs
- A referral → new patient enters SCREENING  
- An ascension → back to DOCTOR for the next tier (from voice agent → grand slam → partnership → SOMA)

## The Compounding Loop

```
LIFE feeds back into the pipeline:

E (Excel) → review → more PSA reach → more SCREENING → more DOCTOR → more LIFE
E (Excel) → referral → skip PSA, enter SCREENING warm → faster DOCTOR → more LIFE
E (Excel) → ascend → back to DOCTOR for next tier → deeper LIFE
```

## LIFE vs Traditional "Customer Success"

| Customer Success | LIFE |
|-----------------|------|
| Quarterly check-in call | Genuine ongoing interest (I) |
| NPS survey | Review earned through results (E) |
| Upsell email | Natural ascension from "what else can you do?" (E) |
| Churn prevention | Love from systems that just work (L) |
| Onboarding | Fulfillment IS the onboarding — it just runs (F) |
