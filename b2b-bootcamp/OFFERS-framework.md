# OFFERS — The Treatment Plan (Inside DOCTOR T)

## The Value Equation (Medical Frame)

Hormozi:  Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)

Medical:  **Value = (Desired Identity × Proven Likelihood) / Cost**

```
        DESIRED IDENTITY × PROVEN LIKELIHOOD
VALUE = ─────────────────────────────────────
        HOW LONG + HOW MUCH + HOW HARD
```

### Desired Identity
Who do you become after treatment? Not "you get a voice agent" — "you become a business that never misses a lead, never sleeps, never forgets follow-up." The identity shift, not the feature.

### Proven Likelihood
Clinical evidence. Other patients' results. The x-ray proof. The benchmarks. "These businesses got X results with WORSE AI. AI is now Y% better." You don't need your own case studies — other people's proof IS clinical evidence.

### Cost (the denominator — minimize this)
- **How long**: "Voice agent live in 48 hours. Grand slam in 2 weeks. Full system in 90 days."
- **How much**: Tiered. Each tier has a price that makes sense against the value math.
- **How hard**: "We do it for you. You don't configure anything. You don't learn anything. You don't manage anything."

## The Tiers (Treatment Plan)

| Tier | Treatment | Desired Identity | Proven Likelihood | Cost |
|------|-----------|-----------------|-------------------|------|
| Antibiotics | Voice agent (1 system) | "Business that captures after-hours leads" | High — proven tech, simple | Low time, low $, zero effort |
| Non-surgical | Grand slam (3 systems) | "Business with complete frontline AI" | Very high — compound effect | Medium time, medium $ |
| Permanent | Partnership + SOMA | "Business that self-improves with AI" | Certain — Isaac embedded | Longer, higher $, but lowest effort (we do everything) |

## The Grand Slam

All three tiers bundled. The "just fix everything" option:

"Look — you can do antibiotics today and come back in 6 months for the operation. Or we can handle everything now, you walk out with the complete system, and we monitor it ongoing. Most patients choose this because they don't want to come back."

## The Guarantee

Medical: "If the treatment doesn't work, we retreat at no additional cost."
Business: "If the system doesn't recover at least X leads in the first 30 days, we refund the setup."

The guarantee reduces the denominator (risk = effort/sacrifice). Lower denominator = higher value.
