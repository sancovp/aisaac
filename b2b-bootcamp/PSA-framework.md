# PSA — The Awareness Broadcast

## What It Is

The thing that gets them to the SCREENING. The ad, the post, the email, the cold DM, the video hook. One-to-many broadcast that catches people who don't know they have a problem.

## Medical Equivalent

Pharma TV ads: "Do you experience fatigue? Joint pain? Ask your doctor about..."
Public health campaigns: "1 in 4 adults has undiagnosed X. Get screened."

## Business Equivalent

"AI Transformation? Again? Yeah but this time it isn't about wrapping Dograh API voice agents in 10 minutes and charging you $20K/mo..."

## The Formula

1. **Pattern interrupt** — acknowledge they've seen this pitch before, break the frame
2. **List symptoms** — things they experience daily but don't recognize as a "condition"
3. **Name the condition** — "you have zero AI systems and it's costing you $X/mo"
4. **Anti-position** — "everyone else selling this can't code, presents prompts as custom, charges $20K for 10 minutes of work"
5. **Invitation** — "come get SCREENED" / "watch this" / "attend the CLINIC"

## PSA vs Ad vs Content

| Traditional Ad | PSA-Style |
|---------------|-----------|
| "Buy our AI agents!" | "You might be losing $20K/mo and not know it" |
| Features + price | Symptoms + condition + invitation |
| Sells the product | Warns about the problem |
| Prospect feels sold to | Prospect feels informed |

## Where It Lives

- LinkedIn posts (60-30-10: this is the 60% value portion)
- Cold emails (CAN-SPAM compliant, "reply no thanks")
- YouTube/video hooks (first 15 seconds)
- Paid ads (Creatify, Meta, LinkedIn)
- Cold DMs to business owners
- The opening of the recorded webinar/CLINIC

## The Anti-Position Hook (Isaac's Script)

"AI TRANSFORMATION!? I know what you're thinking 'this f-ing sh- again?'

Yeah. Again.

But this time it isn't about wrapping Dograh API voice agents in 10 minutes and charging you $20K/mo (yes lol). It's about you acquiring the torch of understanding and donning the armor of information equality — instead of asymmetry, which is what you have now because I know more than you do about how implementing AI in your non-AI business that 'doesn't need' AI works..."

## Success Metric

They show up to the SCREENING. That's it. PSA's only job is to get them in the door.
