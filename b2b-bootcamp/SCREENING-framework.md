# SCREENING — The First Diagnostic

## What It Is

The first hands-on interaction where you determine if they have the condition. Happens AFTER the PSA gets them in the door, BEFORE the full CLINIC/DOCTOR experience.

## Medical Equivalent

Blood pressure check at the pharmacy. Mammogram. Online symptom checker quiz.
Quick, low-commitment, tells you if you need to go deeper.

## Business Equivalent

"How much AI does your business have?" "Zero." "OK."
That's the screening. Three sentences. Condition identified.

## The Formula

1. **Capture symptoms** — "What hurts? Where do leads come from? What happens after hours?"
2. **Run the quick scan** — "How many leads/mo? Response time? Follow-up rate? AI systems count?"
3. **Score** — Zero AI = critical. Some AI = moderate. Full stack = not your ICP.
4. **Route** — Critical → CLINIC. Moderate → nurture. Full stack → wrong patient, refer out.

## SCREENING vs CLINIC

| SCREENING | CLINIC |
|-----------|--------|
| 5 minutes | 1-3 hours (or 3 days) |
| Binary: condition Y/N | Full diagnostic with x-rays |
| Qualifies | Educates + closes |
| "Do you have AI?" | "Here's exactly what it's costing you" |
| Low commitment | High commitment |
| Can be automated (quiz, form, chatbot) | Requires the practitioner |

## Delivery Formats

- **Quiz/form** on landing page: "Score your AI readiness in 60 seconds"
- **Chatbot** on deal room: "Tell me about your business" (qualifies via BANT)
- **First 5 min of webinar**: "Quick show of hands / poll — how many AI systems does your business run?"
- **Cold call opener**: "I help businesses that have zero AI get their first systems. Is that you?"
- **LinkedIn DM**: "Saw your business does X. Quick question — are you running any AI on your lead flow right now?"

## The Lock

The screening locks them into the frame (from the C in CLINIC):

"Your job is specifically to save money, time, and resources for the business while increasing revenue, available labor, and resources. Right? Isn't it? So you're here to see if this creates measurable ROI. Just making sure."

After the lock, they can't escape the CLINIC.

## Success Metric

They enter the CLINIC with a confirmed condition and a locked frame. The SCREENING's job: qualify + lock. Nothing more.

## The Pipeline

```
PSA (broadcast) → catches attention
  ↓
SCREENING (qualify) → "Zero AI? OK."
  ↓
CLINIC (diagnose + close) → DOCTOR protocol
  ↓
LIFE (nurture + compound) → flywheel
```
