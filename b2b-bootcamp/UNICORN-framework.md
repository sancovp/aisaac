## Why AI Changes This

Without AI: scaling to 1M people requires massive teams, infrastructure, management overhead. The cost curve bends against you.

With AI (compiled operations): each additional person costs near-zero to serve because the systems run themselves. The cost curve stays flat. The revenue curve compounds.

## The UNICORN Diagnostic

Three questions that determine the path:

**1. "Is the UNICORN version of your company just the automated version of what you already do?"**

If yes → compile. You have the product, the market, the model. You just need the systems to scale delivery to 1M without scaling headcount. This is the simplest path. CAVE compiles your operations and you ride the curve.

**2. "Are you already there?"**

Some businesses are sitting on UNICORN math and don't know it. They have 50K customers at $30/mo and think that's their ceiling. It's not — they just need to compile the bottleneck (usually fulfillment or support) and the ceiling lifts.

**3. "Or do you need to make higher meta-systems to open more TAM?"**

If your current business model tops out at 10K customers no matter what — you need to go META. Build systems that create new markets, new segments, new TAM. This is where the metacompiler enters: compile your BUSINESS MODEL, not just your operations. Open the aperture. Then compile the operations of the new model.

## The Three Paths to UNICORN

```
PATH A: Compile operations     → "Automate what you do" → ride to 1M
PATH B: Unlock hidden ceiling  → "You're closer than you think" → compile bottleneck
PATH C: Compile the model      → "Go meta" → build systems that open TAM → then compile operations
```

Each path is a different CAVE engagement. The SCREENING tells you which path. The DOCTOR prescribes accordingly.
