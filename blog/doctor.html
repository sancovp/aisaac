Framework

DOCTORThe Closer Script That Feels Like Care

Everyone else uses sales scripts. We use a medical protocol. DOCTOR is a closer framework modeled on how the best doctors actually work: diagnose, show the problem, present tiered treatments, let the patient decide. The result? Clients don't feel sold to. They feel treated.

D — Diagnose

Free discovery session. Scan their business. Take the x-rays. You're the expert examining them, not a salesperson asking questions.

O — Observe

Show them the problem visually. "See this gap? That's where leads disappear." They see it themselves — you don't have to convince them.

C — Consequences

The awful completion of their current trajectory. "If we don't address this, here's what happens in 12 months." Then stop talking. The silence does the closing.

T — Treat

Tiered solutions by certainty level. Quick fix today. Real fix soon. Permanent solution. Each tier is a different level of confidence in the outcome.

O — Options

Financing, payment plans, partnership structures. Remove the money objection structurally — don't argue about price, present options.

R — Resolve

Close and reinforce. "You're making the right choice." Schedule it. They leave feeling cared for, not closed.

Why DOCTOR beats CLOSER: Same psychology, medical frame. CLOSER feels like sales. DOCTOR feels like care. The prospect doesn't think "I'm being closed." They think "I'm being treated."
