## Part 3: The Smart Play

- You want to build big complex systems (the real thing)

- Simple systems = the information asymmetry plays (voice agents, content, lead gen)
- Automating them funds and teaches you to build the complex ones
- Each simple system you automate becomes a product you sell AND a lesson you internalize

**Source material:** Holographic Work, The System Audit
