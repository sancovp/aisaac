// Services

What we stop from going wrong.

Every job is what it prevents. Water damage. Sewage in the basement. A water heater that floods the garage at midnight. We don't just fix things — we make sure the worst-case scenario never happens.

01

Water Heaters

Tank, tankless, hybrid heat pump. Repair, replace, maintain. We work on all makes and models, no warranty games.

→ Prevents: Cold showers, flooded garages, $8K water damage
02

Drain & Sewer

Snaking, hydro jetting, camera inspection. We find the blockage, we clear it, we show you the before/after on video.

→ Prevents: Backed-up drains, sewage in the basement, yard excavation
03

Leak Detection

Slab leaks, supply line tracing, mystery water bills. Acoustic + thermal imaging. Surgical repair, not demo.

→ Prevents: Foundation damage, mold, $20K+ water bills

Whole-Home Repipe

Copper, PEX, polybutylene replacement. We replace aging supply lines before they fail — on your timeline, not the pipe's.

→ Prevents: Pipe bursts, low pressure, water contamination

Gas Lines

New gas line installation, leak repair, appliance hookups. CSST-certified. Pressure tested, documented, permitted.

→ Prevents: Gas leaks, CO poisoning, fire

Commercial

Backflow testing, grease line maintenance, RPZ certification. Restaurants, offices, multi-family. Scheduled service contracts.

→ Prevents: Health code violations, water contamination, business interruption
