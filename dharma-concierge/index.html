About
Isaac, Acharya

I'm Isaac — a Buddhist Acharya in the Shambhala warrior tradition, trained under Ngakchang Karma Yeshe Namgyal Dorje Rinpoche. I wrote Completely Fake Dharma because too many meditation groups and dharma centers are watered down by poorly integrated Western ideas — mindfulness without the path, relaxation without realization.

Dharma Concierge exists to provide authentic practice supports — starting with the Black Manjushri Chulen that has supported practitioners for over a century.

The original text describes this chulen as a support for windhorse — the vital energy of practice. It can enhance Tantric fasting and essence extraction, help untie subtle knots in the energy body through Tsa/Lung/Tigle (Tummo), or simply deepen one's sitting practice. Offered solely as a traditional ritual support.
