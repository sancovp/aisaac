About

Isaac, Acharya

I'm Isaac — a Buddhist Acharya in the Shambhala warrior tradition, trained under Ngakchang Karma Yeshe Namgyal Dorje Rinpoche. I wrote Completely Fake Dharma because too many meditation groups are watered down by poorly integrated Western ideas — mindfulness without the path, relaxation without realization.

Dharma Concierge exists to provide authentic practice supports — starting with the Black Manjushri Chulen that has supported practitioners for over a century.

Spiritual Director

Ordained in 1993 as a Ngakpa Lama by Karma Thinley Rinpoche IV. A Western Lama who, for more than half a century, has practiced and taught Lord Shakyamuni Buddha's teachings.
