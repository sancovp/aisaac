Heart Aspiration to the Emanation Line

Written February 25, 2022 — an invocation of the unbroken lineage from Kuntuzangpo through the masters of Do Ngak Dzong.

Kuntuzangpo and Kuntuzangmo,
1000 Buddhas of this fortunate aeon,
With all the marks and signs,
Great wisdom, Great compassion, Great power…

Long Life Prayer for Rinpoche

Written February 20, 2020 — a tenshug (long-life prayer) for the Secret Yogi, Wielder of Mantric Power, Action Wisdom Victory Vajra.

Om Svasti!
Secretly, you are the iron bridge building mad man
But in this life you are more like the King of the Vast Open Road

Dharma Talks

Rinpoche has personally selected and introduced these teachings. Each carries his endorsement and commentary for the Do Ngak Dzong sangha.

01
H.H. 14th Dalai Lama

The Four Noble Truths

A landmark teaching contrasting the Theravada Pali tradition with the Indian Sanskrit Nalanda approach. Deeply extrapolates on Shunyata, the Two Truths, Nagarjuna, Dharmakirti, and Bodhicitta — paired with the Dalai Lama's breadth across quantum physics and world affairs.

→
02
Lama Tharchin Rinpoche

Four Thoughts That Turn the Mind

The foundational Vajrayana preliminary teaching from the Repkong lineage — precious human birth, impermanence, karma, the nature of suffering. Rinpoche shares his personal closeness to Lama Tharchin and the transmission he received on meditation and openness.

→
03
Lama Tharchin Rinpoche

Kunzang Monlam — Prayer of Samantabhadra

A Dzogchen chanting practice from the terma of Rigdzin Godem (14th century). Use Lama Tharchin's recording as your guide for recitation. One of the most profound prayers of the Nyingma tradition.

→
04
Khandro Rinpoche

Compassion vs. Competition

Great practical upadesa bridging Eastern and Western perspectives on compassion — how it grows through time, and how competition is its shadow. Khandro Rinpoche is a daughter of Mindroling Trichen, one of the throne-holders of the Nyingma school.

→
