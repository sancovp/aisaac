## Key Frameworks & Insights

### 1. "Positioning Over Tactics"
The #1 mistake AI agency builders make: leading with the tool (n8n, Make, Claude) instead of the transformation. Clients don't care about MCP servers. They care about "your business runs itself while you sleep."

### 2. "AI Operating System" Framing
Don't sell individual automations. Sell an "AI operating system" that runs the entire business. This justifies $10K+ pricing because it replaces multiple hires, not just one task.

### 3. Mindset + Structure > Information
> "Most people don't fail because they lack information, they fail because they don't have structure, accountability, or the belief that they can actually do it."

His program is 50% technical, 50% entrepreneurial mindset — rejection handling, confidence, sales psychology.

### 4. From Tool Builder to Strategic Architect
The progression: hobbyist -> tool builder -> agency operator -> strategic AI architect. Each level commands higher prices. The "co-founder" framing is the final level — Claude is not your employee, it is your business partner that handles operations.

### 5. Claude Code Specifically
- MCP servers are the key differentiator (custom integrations per client)
- Scheduled autonomous tasks (night-mode operations)
- Cowork for team access (not just solo developer tool)
- The system compounds: once built, it improves itself through usage data
