## Overview

Leon shows how he generated $5,219 in 24 hours selling a digital PDF guide about parenting/sleep solutions. The entire pipeline uses Claude AI for content creation, Facebook ads with lookalike audiences for distribution, and a Shopify store for conversion. He emphasizes this is not magic — it's a repeatable system built on emotion-based product selection, influencer seeding, pixel warming, and lookalike audience scaling.

## The Complete Funnel (Step by Step)

### Step 1: Find a Trending Emotional Product Idea
- **Tool:** PDF Trend Lab (paid tool, link in his description)
- **Method:** Select a niche (he chose "parenting"), click "generate ideas"
- **How it works:** Pulls real-time search data from Google, Reddit, Twitter to find problems people are actively searching to resolve
- **Critical insight:** Sell **emotion-based** guides. Nobody buys "how to brush your teeth." People buy solutions to pain points they're actively struggling with.
- **His pick:** Sleep solutions for parents — "Parents Hate a Choppy Sleeper: How to Address Sleep Issues and Fix It for Good"

### Step 2: Create the PDF Guide with Claude AI
- **Tool:** Claude AI
- **Prompt pattern:** "Write me an entire PDF guide for [title + subtitle]. Make it full of actionable steps based on real life experiences to help the potential buyer."
- Claude writes the entire guide in chat
- He reviews it to ensure it actually helps with the problem (even a little bit)
- **Price point:** $9 to $27 — low enough that buyers don't think twice, low enough that returns are minimal ($125 returned out of $5,219)

### Step 3: Design the Guide
- **Tool:** Canva for layout/design
- **Tool:** Unsplash for free copyright-free cover images
- Copy/paste Claude's output into Canva pages, design it to look professional
- He made the title shorter and more "punchy" than what PDF Trend Lab generated (10 years of marketing experience helped, but you can ask Claude to make titles punchier too)

### Step 4: Build a Shopify Store
- **Why Shopify over Etsy/Gumroad:** Full customization creates a better buying experience, which increases conversion rate
- His conversion rate: **3.67%** (he calls this "pretty high")
- **Why not Etsy:** You compete with sellers who have tens of thousands of reviews. Your listing won't pop.
- **Why not Gumroad:** Can't customize the experience enough

### Step 5: Seed with Influencer Marketing (BEFORE Facebook Ads)
- **Critical sequence:** Influencer marketing FIRST, then Facebook ads
- **Why YouTube influencers specifically:** People spend 27 minutes with a YouTuber per video vs. 15 seconds on TikTok. Engagement and trust are vastly higher.
- **His method:** Reached out to a YouTuber in the parenting niche who gets 100K+ views
- **Payment:** $700 upfront + 15% affiliate commission
- **Result:** This drove the initial $15,000 in 30 days (from previous video) AND warmed his Facebook pixel

### Step 6: Warm the Facebook Pixel
- **What a pixel is:** Code on your Shopify store that tracks every visitor action (page view, view content, add to cart, initiate checkout, purchase)
- **Why influencer traffic matters:** All those thousands of visitors from the YouTube influencer's audience train the pixel on what your ideal buyer looks like
- The pixel gets "warmer and warmer" as more data flows through
- Instead of showing ads to "random Jeff in Utah," Facebook learns who actually buys

### Step 7: Create Custom Audiences
- Go to Facebook Ads Manager > All Tools > Audiences > Create Custom Audience > Website
- Create SEPARATE custom audiences for each event:
  - All website visitors
  - Page view
  - View content
  - Add to cart
- Set time window to 180 days for maximum data
- Name them clearly (e.g., "ATC 180 days")
- Refresh batches every 30-60-90 days

### Step 8: Create Lookalike Audiences (THE KEY MOVE)
- From each custom audience, create a **lookalike audience**
- Facebook generates **millions** of people who look like your custom audience
- Set lookalike percentage to **5%** (Leon's sweet spot from 10 years of testing — 1% is hottest but smallest)
- This is the magic: instead of targeting cold "parents with children ages 3-5" (generic), you target millions of people who LOOK LIKE the people who already bought from you

### Step 9: Run Facebook Ads Targeting Lookalikes
- Create campaign > Sales objective
- Instead of detail targeting (cold), use Custom Audiences and select your lookalike audiences
- Name ad sets by what you're targeting so you know what's working
- Result: $5,219 in 24 hours with 3.67% conversion rate
- He mentions retargeting ads have even more insane ROAS but saves that for another video

## Key Frameworks & Principles

### The Warm Traffic Ladder
```
Influencer (YouTube) seeds warm traffic
  -> Pixel tracks all visitor behavior
    -> Custom audiences capture buyer signals
      -> Lookalike audiences scale to millions
        -> Facebook ads convert at high rates
```

### Emotion-Based Product Selection
- Don't sell information people already know
- Sell solutions to problems causing emotional distress RIGHT NOW
- Use real-time search data to validate demand exists
- Price at impulse-buy level ($9-$27) to minimize friction and returns

### The Influencer-to-Pixel Pipeline
- Influencer marketing is NOT the endgame — it's the SEED
- Its real value is training the Facebook pixel with high-quality buyer data
- Once the pixel is warm, Facebook ads take over and scale far beyond what influencers can do
- $700 + 15% commission for influencer -> then Facebook scales to $5K/day

### Why YouTube Influencers > TikTok/Instagram
- Time spent per video = trust built
- 27 min YouTube video > 200 TikTok clips for same engagement
- Higher quality traffic that converts better
