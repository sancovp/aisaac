# ASCEND-MAP — the conversion map: every gap the free layer deliberately leaves open

For each free piece: the **class-shape it hands over** (the genuine value), the **gap it leaves open**
(the operational/instantiation rung it withholds — what the reader can now *see* but can't yet
*build*), and **what ascending gives.** This is the conversion map. The gaps are not manufactured —
each one is the true distance between holding a shape and being able to instantiate it. That honesty
is what makes the gap convert.

The mechanism, restated: a reader who finishes the free layer is *holding the class-level shape* of
how a real compound-AI system thinks, and *lacks the language to forward-chain and fill the args to
instantiate it.* They can see the destination; they can't build the path. Seeing that gap → realizing
the operational language is the thing Isaac has and they don't → trust → ascend.

## Frameworks

### `frameworks/the-four-slots.md`
- **Hands over:** any complex system reduces to steer / carry / align / loops, and the empty slot is
  the bug.
- **Gap left open:** the machinery that *enforces* the slots once named — the structures that fire the
  alignment check on a schedule, make handoffs binding, and hold all four under pressure automatically.
- **Ascending gives:** the operational harness that keeps a real system (business or agent) from
  drifting *by construction*, not by remembering to look.

### `frameworks/build-in-verified-layers.md`
- **Hands over:** advance only on an observable/independent signal; collapse is skipping it;
  completion grants vision of the next layer.
- **Gap left open:** the operational definition of "the signal" *for a specific system*, and the
  verification gates that make the layers self-verifying.
- **Ascending gives:** the instrumented gates that make a build refuse to advance on an absent signal —
  the thing that separates compounding systems from collapsing ones.

### `frameworks/right-is-not-fluent.md`
- **Hands over:** "right" = every claim composes from validated parts; typed structure is what makes
  "right" definable; more context can't close the gap.
- **Gap left open:** the apparatus that does the composing-and-refusing — how claims get decomposed and
  checked at every depth, the type system that gates admission, the repair messages.
- **Ascending gives:** an AI that is *structurally unable* to admit a wrong claim about your domain —
  the upper rung that can't be faked with a weekend.

### `frameworks/drift-is-the-default.md`
- **Hands over:** stochastic systems drift; correctness is a short-interval *return*; frequency-of-
  return is the real metric.
- **Gap left open:** the machinery that performs the return *without you* — schedule-fired checks,
  drift detected from the work itself, corrections injected so the next step re-aligns invisibly.
- **Ascending gives:** unsupervised multi-hour/multi-day agent runs that stay on the goal and compound
  instead of degenerating.

### `frameworks/forge-your-own-vocabulary.md`
- **Hands over:** your mind reaches for cross-domain analogies; triangulating them collapses out a
  nameable universal; the name becomes reusable equipment.
- **Gap left open:** doing it *reliably and at depth* — producing a precise typed reusable shape (not a
  vague metaphor), and installing a forged framework so it conditions an AI turn after turn and
  *accumulates*.
- **Ascending gives:** a repeatable forge whose output is durable, composable equipment that compounds.

### `frameworks/the-altitude-ladder.md`
- **Hands over:** the engineering and economic ladders are the same ladder; most are lower than they
  think; low rungs are commoditized; the top is a person.
- **Gap left open:** the path *up* — the disciplines and language that move you from configuring to
  engineering to building-the-thing-that-builds-systems.
- **Ascending gives:** the actual climb — or the relationship with someone who is the top rung for your
  domain. (This piece is the purest statement of the whole funnel's mechanism: see the destination,
  can't yet build the path.)

### `frameworks/compounds-or-rots.md`
- **Hands over:** every continuous system compounds or rots; the fork is self-readability; membranes
  beat walls; the engine is one thing at many resolutions.
- **Gap left open:** the machinery of the compounding loop — automatic detection of hidden effects,
  self-repair of explanatory surfaces, a methodology encoded *as computation that can't drift*.
- **Ascending gives:** a system that gets better the more it's used, because it repairs its own
  observability every cycle.

## Case studies (each leaks that a deeper operational rung EXISTS, withholds the language)

### `case-studies/the-compiler-that-resolves-the-ordinary.md`
- **Reveals exists:** a resolution compiler whose output is typed-and-composing structure (not prose),
  that compounds across runs and closes a self-feeding loop.
- **Gap left open:** the fields, the typed schema, the graph-commit mechanism, the chain-closes check.
- **Ascending gives:** the ability to build a compiler whose outputs are valid by construction.

### `case-studies/the-validator-that-refuses-to-be-wrong.md`
- **Reveals exists:** a compositional validator that gates the AI's claims against a built-up typed
  model and turns rejections into repair instructions.
- **Gap left open:** the claim syntax, the ontology and its restrictions, the depth-wise
  decomposition/check, the quarantine-and-repair cycle.
- **Ascending gives:** an AI that structurally can't be wrong about your specific domain.

### `case-studies/the-agent-that-only-sees-the-next-turn.md`
- **Reveals exists:** a harness that shows only the current step and *blocks* invalid moves, flipping
  the failure mode from silent-wrong-output to clean-signal.
- **Gap left open:** the layered architecture, the waypoint form, the guard wiring, the corrective-
  message structure.
- **Ascending gives:** reliable multi-step agents that forward-chain without babysitting.

### `case-studies/the-missing-slot-found-in-an-afternoon.md`
- **Reveals exists:** a four-slot diagnosis (forged via cross-domain analogy) that instantly locates
  the missing coordination layer. *(Also hands over the diagnostic itself — the strongest free lever.)*
- **Gap left open:** *installing and enforcing* the alignment check so drift is caught automatically,
  not by willpower; making handoffs binding in a running system.
- **Ascending gives:** the structure that keeps all four slots filled under pressure so the system
  can't drift in the first place.

### `case-studies/the-repo-that-repaired-its-own-blind-spots.md`
- **Reveals exists:** a self-readability repair loop that surfaces invisible coupling and makes each
  pass safer — a concrete compounding loop.
- **Gap left open:** the coupling categories to detect, the marker form/placement, the automatic
  detect-and-patch pass and its wiring.
- **Ascending gives:** a system that gets safer the more it's edited, on the compounding trajectory.

### `case-studies/the-ten-minute-product-priced-like-magic.md`
- **Reveals exists:** the whole board — the levels ladder, the markup-on-the-knowledge-gap, the
  give-the-map-away move. *(The clearest instance of the funnel mechanism.)*
- **Gap left open:** the path *up* — the language and disciplines of the upper rungs the companion
  frameworks describe only the *shape* of.
- **Ascending gives:** the actual ability to operate at the durable rungs, or a guide who already does.

## The strongest conversion levers (where the gap bites hardest)

1. **"Right is not fluent."** The reader leaves *certain* their AI only performs knowing, *certain*
   that more context won't fix it, and *able to see* that a system where wrong claims can't be admitted
   is real — but with **zero language for how the composing-and-refusing is actually done.** They can
   now feel the exact shape of the thing they can't build. This is the deepest, most honest gap: the
   one upper rung that genuinely can't be faked, sitting in full view.

2. **"The four slots" + the missing-slot case study.** The reader gets a *working* two-minute
   diagnostic that finds the bug in their own system — real, immediate value that proves the altitude
   is genuine — and the bug it finds is almost always "nothing keeps this aligned." They can now *see*
   the missing piece precisely, and discover they have no idea how to *install and enforce* the thing
   that fills it. Maximum "I can see the destination, I can't build the path."

3. **"The altitude ladder" + the ten-minute-product case study.** Together they show the whole board
   and locate the reader on it honestly — then leave the path *up* the ladder entirely open. The reader
   ends up holding the map, seeing exactly where durable value lives, and lacking the language to climb
   there. The gap is the ladder itself, in plain sight.
