Department

🦷Dental Health

Teeth, gums, jaw. The system that files the loudest complaints when ignored.

Risk signals

What to watch.

Dental problems announce themselves with pain. By the time pain arrives, the damage is structural. Watch the precursors.

Bleeding gums

Gums bleed when brushing or flossing. Early periodontal signal. Not normal. Never normal.

Jaw tension / grinding

Bruxism. Waking up with sore jaw. Worn enamel. Stress manifesting as structural damage.

Sensitivity

Hot/cold sensitivity increasing over time. Enamel erosion or cavity forming underneath.

Breath changes

Persistent bad breath despite hygiene. Bacterial load or deeper infection signal.

Professional routing

When to call the specialist.

Dentist + Periodontist (if gum issues)

Cadence: Every 6 months

Trigger: Any bleeding, pain, sensitivity change, or visible damage

Dental is the easiest department to maintain and the most expensive to repair. A $200 cleaning prevents a $5,000 root canal.
