Department

🌍Environmental

A standing desk is an environment patch, not a health identity.

Risk signals

What to watch.

Your environment shapes your health defaults. Bad ergonomics, poor air quality, toxic exposure — these compound silently because you adapt to them.

Workspace ergonomics

Monitor height, chair support, keyboard position. Where is the pain coming from?

Air quality

CO2 levels in closed rooms cause cognitive decline within hours. Ventilate or measure.

Light exposure

Not enough natural light = circadian disruption. Too much blue light = sleep disruption.

Noise baseline

Constant background noise elevates cortisol without you noticing.

Professional routing

When to call the specialist.

Self-audit / Occupational therapist

Cadence: Quarterly self-audit

Trigger: Persistent headaches, brain fog in specific locations, respiratory issues at home/work

Most environmental health fixes cost under $100: a CO2 monitor, a monitor arm, a better chair. The ROI is measured in decades of pain avoided.
