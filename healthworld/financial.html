Department

💰Financial Health

Money stress is physiological stress. Same cortisol. Same damage.

Risk signals

What to watch.

Financial precarity produces the same chronic stress response as physical danger. Your body cannot tell the difference between a predator and an overdraft.

Cashflow visibility

Do you know, right now, what comes in and goes out this month? If not, you're flying blind.

Debt-to-income ratio

Above 36% = financial stress zone. Above 50% = crisis territory.

Emergency runway

Months of expenses in savings. Under 3 months = one event away from cascade.

Income source concentration

One employer = one point of failure. Multiple streams = resilience.

Professional routing

When to call the specialist.

Fee-only Financial Advisor / CPA

Cadence: Quarterly review

Trigger: Debt increasing, no emergency fund, major life change (job, house, child), tax complexity

Fee-only means they get paid by you, not by selling you products. This distinction matters. Commission-based advisors have misaligned incentives by design.
