Department

🧬Gut & Digestion

Second brain. Treats complaints as data, not inconvenience.

Risk signals

What to watch.

The gut communicates through discomfort, energy, mood, and skin. Most people normalize the signals because they've always been there.

Bloating patterns

Which foods trigger it? How long after eating? Bloating is data, not a personality trait.

Energy after meals

Crashing after lunch is not normal. It's a glycemic or food sensitivity signal.

Bowel regularity

Changes in frequency, consistency, or color. Bristol scale is the metric.

Skin breakouts

Gut-skin axis. Persistent acne or rashes that don't respond to topical treatment = look at the gut.

Professional routing

When to call the specialist.

Gastroenterologist

Cadence: As needed (trigger-based)

Trigger: Persistent bloating, blood in stool, unexplained weight change, chronic fatigue after meals

Gut issues masquerade as everything: fatigue, brain fog, skin problems, mood disorders. If three departments are struggling and you can't find the cause, check the gut.
