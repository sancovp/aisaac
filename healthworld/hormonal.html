⚗️Hormonal

The invisible CEO that controls every other department. Check the bloodwork.

Risk signals

What to watch.

Hormones set the baseline for energy, mood, body composition, libido, and recovery. You can optimize every other department and still feel terrible if this one is off.

Persistent fatigue

Sleeping enough, eating right, still exhausted. Thyroid or testosterone.

Body composition shift

Gaining fat, losing muscle despite training. Hormonal metabolism shift.

Mood/libido changes

Both declining together over months. Not aging. Hormonal.

Temperature sensitivity

Always cold or always hot when others aren't. Thyroid marker.

Professional routing

When to call the specialist.

Endocrinologist / Functional Medicine

Cadence: Annual bloodwork panel

Trigger: Persistent fatigue + mood change + body composition shift (the triad)

Reference ranges on bloodwork are population averages, not optimal levels. An endocrinologist who treats to optimal rather than reference can make a measurable difference.
