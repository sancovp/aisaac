How it works

Osmosis Jones your life.

Each department gets four things. That is the entire system.

Visibility

What are the signals? What numbers should you know? You cannot manage what you cannot see. Most health failures start as invisible signals that got normalized.

Maintenance

What do you do daily, weekly, monthly? Not aspirationally. Actually. The maintenance schedule is the floor. Everything above it is upside.

Professional Routing

Which specialist handles this department? What triggers a visit? When is the cadence? Professional routing removes the "I should probably see someone" loop that never closes.

Identity Adoption

Do you identify as someone who maintains this? A standing desk is environment. A person who tracks their posture is identity. The difference between L2 and L3 is identity.

Integration

Same engine. Different world.

CAVE Automations

Cron-driven health checks. Morning journal scores. Supplement reminders. Appointment booking triggers. The system runs while you live.
