🛡️Skin & Dermatology

The boundary layer. External signals of internal state.

Risk signals

What to watch.

Skin is the body's largest organ and its most visible status indicator. Persistent issues that don't respond to topical treatment are pointing inward.

New or changing moles

ABCDE rule: Asymmetry, Border, Color, Diameter, Evolution. Skin cancer screening.

Persistent acne

Adult acne after 25 = hormonal, gut, or stress signal. Not a skincare product problem.

Dryness/eczema patterns

Where does it flare? When? Stress, diet, or environmental trigger.

Slow wound healing

Cuts taking longer to heal. Nutritional deficit or metabolic signal.

Professional routing

When to call the specialist.

Dermatologist

Cadence: Annual screening

Trigger: New/changing moles, rash >2 weeks, unexplained hair loss, nail changes

Dermatologists are booked 3-6 months out in most cities. Schedule the annual screening now. When something urgent appears, you'll already have a relationship.
