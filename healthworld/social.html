Department

🤝Social & Relationship

Loneliness is a mortality factor. Not a feeling. A factor.

Risk signals

What to watch.

Social isolation has the mortality impact of smoking 15 cigarettes a day. This is not a metaphor. It is epidemiological data.

Days since real conversation

Not work calls. Not texts. Face-to-face or voice-to-voice with someone who knows you.

Support network size

If you had a crisis at 2am, how many people could you call? The number matters.

Relationship maintenance

Are you maintaining connections or letting them decay? Entropy is the default.

Conflict avoidance

Avoiding necessary conversations. Relationship debt compounds like financial debt.

Maintenance

The floor.

This is not aspirational. This is the minimum viable maintenance schedule. Everything above this is upside.

CadenceAction
DailyOne real interaction. Not transactional. Not digital-only.
WeeklyReach out to one person you haven't talked to this month.
MonthlyEvaluate: am I avoiding anyone? Why?
AnnuallyRelationship audit: who do I want more of? Less of? Why?
Professional routing

When to call the specialist.

Therapist / Couples counselor

Cadence: As needed

Social health is the department most people skip because it feels optional. It is not optional. It is infrastructure.

Stat buffs

What you gain.

Maintaining this department doesn't just prevent failure. It compounds into measurable gains.

+Stress buffering +Longevity +Emotional regulation +Purpose +Accountability

Make this department green.
