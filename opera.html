SANCREV OPERA

The unified interface for compound intelligence. SANCREV OPERA brings together every component — Jobworld (business operations), GNOSYS (AI agent), CartON (knowledge graph), SANCTUM (life architecture), STARSYSTEM (development) — into one application.

One install. One interface. Everything compounds.

Jobworld
AI-powered business operations. Departments, agents, SOPs, automations. Your company runs itself.
GNOSYS
Personal AI agent. Builds skills, accumulates knowledge, codes, researches, publishes. Your compound intelligence partner.
CartON
Knowledge graph. 190K+ concepts, semantic search, typed projections. Everything you learn persists and connects.
SANCTUM
Life architecture. Health, wealth, relationships, purpose, growth, environment. The system that keeps the human intact.
STARSYSTEM
Development infrastructure. Flights, missions, starlog, waypoints. How the system builds and evolves itself.

Status: Building in the open

SANCREV OPERA is under active development. The components exist and run individually. The unified interface is coming. Follow the build on Isaac's blog and Patreon.
