Under the hood

What's actually in this thing?

OPERA is not one program. It's a compound intelligence system — multiple layers that each do one job and make each other stronger. Here's what runs when you start it.

Conductor

The remote control. Sees EVERY agent running — behind GNOSYS, on top of GNOSYS, alongside GNOSYS. The total observation and control interface. Available 24/7 on Discord.

GNOSYS

Your Personal AI Infrastructure Agent (PAIA). Made by OPERA. Builds skills, writes code, accumulates knowledge, produces content, manages operations. The AI that knows YOU.

Jobworld

The UNICORN interface. Business dashboards, department views, SOPs, automations. What you see when you look at your company. What your clients see when they look at theirs.

Paperclip

Agent orchestration (MIT, open source). Org charts, budgets, heartbeats, governance. The CEO agent decomposes goals and hires department heads. We plug our brain underneath.

CAVE

Our agent runtime. Like OpenClaw, but with ontological validation, knowledge graph backing, and logic enforcement. Every agent runs on CAVE. Every automation is a CAVE automation.

CartON

Knowledge graph. 190K+ concepts. Semantic search. Typed relationships. The intelligence that compounds between your personal and business domains — forever.
