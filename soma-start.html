FAQ

What channels can I use to observe?
Any messaging platform — iMessage, Discord, Slack, WhatsApp, the SOMA mobile app, or direct API. The system meets you where you already communicate.

How long until I see automations?
Basic pattern recognition starts within days. Fuller automation follows as the system observes more of your work — the more consistent the observation, the faster it compounds. Domain-specialized editions help here.

Is my data private?
Your observations are yours. Domain improvement from opted-in data is anonymized and aggregated — individual processes are never shared. Enterprise tier includes on-premise deployment options.

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly billing, cancel anytime. Your knowledge graph exports with you.
