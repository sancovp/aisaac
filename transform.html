The game

L1

Prompts

Say the right thing to AI. This is what agencies sell. It takes 10 minutes.

L2

Tools

Give AI actions — CRM, calendar, email. Useful. Still configuration.

L3

Context

Show AI the right information. Knowledge bases, RAG, documents. Now it knows your business.
