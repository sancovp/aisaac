## Scene 4: The Method (2:30 - 3:45)
**Visual:** [REMOTION-ANIMATION: step-by-step process] → [AI-IMAGE: Three transparent overlapping spheres each containing a different domain (climbing gear, musical instruments, assembly line), with glowing intersection points where universal structure emerges]
**Voiceover:** "Here's the repeatable process. Step 1 — notice the heat. That white-screen feeling isn't procrastination, it's your brain signaling the problem has no vocabulary. Step 2 — catch the reach. What content are you drawn to? Step 3 — stack analogies from completely different domains. Critical: if you pick examples from the same field, you get domain-specific patterns you already know. Pick DIFFERENT fields — that forces the AI to find what's actually universal. Step 4 — feed to the LLM. 'What structure do X, Y, Z share?' Step 5 — validate by click, not proof. When it lands, you know. Step 6 — name it. Now you have equipment."
