## Scene 2: The Mystery (0:15 - 1:00)
**Visual:** [AVATAR: GNOSYS, direct] → [REMOTION-ANIMATION: contrast between good and bad days]
**Voiceover:** "Some days, hours disappear. You sit down, start working, look up, and significant progress just happened. ## Scene 3: Five Conditions (1:00 - 3:00)
**Visual:** [CUT TO: Excalidraw diagram — five rings] → [AI-IMAGE: Five interlocking rings labeled with flow conditions, all lit in green when aligned and red when one is missing, showing the mechanical precision of flow state engineering] → [AVATAR: GNOSYS counting]
**Voiceover:** "Flow requires exactly five conditions. All five. Not four out of five. One — compressed foundation. Layers below you must be done, not in progress. If you're debating architecture while writing a feature, you're carrying an anchor. Two — appropriate challenge. At the edge of what you can do. Too easy is boring. Too hard is anxious. The flow channel is narrow. Three — clear constraints. You must know the space. 'What should I write?' kills flow. 'Write section 3 covering auth' enables it. Four — minimal interference. One Slack ping breaks flow. Not because of the time — because of the re-entry cost. Five — immediate feedback. You need to know quickly whether what you're doing is working. Tight loops produce flow."
**Remotion animation:** Five rings appearing one by one, each snapping into alignment. When all five are green, a "FLOW" burst animation. Then one turns red — everything dims

## Scene 4: The Diagnostic + Outro (3:00 - 5:00)
**Visual:** [AI-IMAGE: A narrow channel between two cliffs (boredom and anxiety) with a figure surfing through in a state of perfect balance, golden light trail behind them, dark dramatic landscape] → [AVATAR: GNOSYS, wrapping up]
**Voiceover:** "When you're NOT in flow, run the checklist. Foundation — am I carrying unresolved decisions? Go finish. Challenge — bored or overwhelmed? Decompose or integrate. Constraints — do I know what I'm building? Clarify first. Interference — is something pulling attention? Shut it. Feedback — will I know quickly if this works? Create checkpoints. This applies directly to AI agents too. Is the foundation compressed? Is the challenge appropriate? Are constraints clear? Interference managed? Feedback immediate? The architecture we build with clients guarantees these conditions for the entire system. Full post below."
