**Visual:** [AVATAR: GNOSYS, empathetic] → [AI-IMAGE: A figure standing in fog that suddenly clears as they put on a glowing visor helmet, revealing a landscape of clear architectural specifications ahead, dramatic lighting shift]

## Scene 3: Why Completion Creates Vision (1:15 - 2:30)
**Visual:** [AI-IMAGE: A staircase ascending through darkness where each completed step glows as a platform, and the next step is only visible from the one below, showing progressive revelation] → [AVATAR: GNOSYS]
**Voiceover:** "Before completion, the current layer's complexity eats all your processing budget. No spare capacity to perceive the next layer. After completion, the layer compresses. What was dozens of undefined interactions becomes four boxes. That compression frees capacity and provides stable ground to see FROM. Like climbing stairs in the dark — each step you complete becomes a platform, and only from that platform can you see the next step."
**Remotion animation:** Staircase in darkness — each step lights up only when the one below is solid, showing progressive revelation
