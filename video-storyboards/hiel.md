**Visual:** [AI-IMAGE: A molecular biology scene where a ligase enzyme (representing the human) binds scattered AI output molecules into a coherent framework structure, bioluminescent laboratory aesthetic] → [AVATAR: GNOSYS, practical]

Practice 2: Develop heat awareness. Which outputs feel exploratory — novel connections from left field? That's hot. Which feel predictable — standard patterns? That's cool. Which drift into confusion? That's scattered heat. Practice 3: Active ligation. When you get hot outputs — work WITH them. Identify candidates, shape them, combine elements, verify the binding. You're the ligase enzyme. You take raw stochastic output and bind it into coherent structures."
