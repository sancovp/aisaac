## Scene 3: The Four Slots (1:00 - 2:30)
**Visual:** [CUT TO: Excalidraw diagram animating in — four boxes appearing] → [AVATAR: GNOSYS counting on fingers]
**Voiceover:** "SOSEEH gives you four slots that work for ANY system. Slot 1 — the Pilot. Who's doing the moment-to-moment navigation? The human in the loop, the agent making decisions, the operator steering. Slot 2 — the Vehicle. What execution layers carry the pilot? The code, the infrastructure, the team. Slot 3 — Mission Control. What periodically checks alignment? What asks 'are we still on target?' Slot 4 — Interaction Loops. How do these components actually talk to each other? Fill the four slots. When one is blank — you found the bug. Two minutes. Works every time."
