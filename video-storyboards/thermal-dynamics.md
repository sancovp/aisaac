## Scene 3: The Heat Model (1:15 - 2:30)
**Visual:** [CUT TO: Excalidraw diagram animating — heat zones] → [AVATAR: GNOSYS pointing to zones]
**Voiceover:** "Your brain has thermal zones. Green — flowing, productive, sustainable. Yellow — straining but functional. Orange — approaching overload, quality dropping. Red — white-screen. System shutdown. And here's the key — four cooling mechanisms exist. Cathartic cooling — emotional release, talking it out. Structural cooling — decomposing the problem into smaller pieces. Temporal cooling — taking a break, letting heat dissipate. Delegational cooling — giving part of the load to someone else. AI is delegational cooling. It takes the thermal load off your stack."
**Remotion animation:** Four-zone gauge (green/yellow/orange/red) with four cooling arrows pointing downward labeled with each mechanism
